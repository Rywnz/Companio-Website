How Companio Helps

Connect With People

Match with seniors who share your hobbies and interests. Chat, video call, and build real friendships in a safe environment.

Emergency Services

One-tap access to emergency contacts, medical alert services, and family notifications when you need help fast.

Order Essentials

Order groceries, medicine, and everyday items delivered to your door. Simple, reliable, and designed for seniors.

Interest Matching

Discover events, groups, and companions based on what you love — from gardening and books to music and walking groups.

Why Companio?

We believe no one should feel lonely or helpless. Companio brings together friendship, safety, and convenience so elders can live independently while staying connected.

  • Meaningful Connections — Meet people who genuinely share your interests
  • Safety First — Quick access to emergency services and family alerts
  • Easy Ordering — Get what you need without leaving home
  • Community Driven — Local events, groups, and support networks

Companio on the Go

Our mobile app makes it even easier for seniors to stay connected, safe, and independent — right from their phone.

  • Simple Interface — Large text, clear buttons, and voice-guided navigation designed for older users.
  • Instant Alerts — Get notified about community events, messages from friends, and emergency updates.
  • One-Tap Ordering — Reorder your favourite groceries and essentials with a single tap.
  • Video Calls — Chat face-to-face with family, friends, and companions from anywhere.
