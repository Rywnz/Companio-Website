About Companio

Learn about our mission to connect, protect, and empower seniors in our community.

Our Mission

Companio was created with a simple belief: no one should feel lonely or helpless. We're building a platform where seniors can connect with like-minded people, access emergency services when needed, and easily order everyday essentials — all from one trusted place.

We partner with local communities and healthcare providers to ensure every member feels supported, safe, and valued.

Our Story

Companio started when our founder noticed their elderly neighbour struggling with isolation and everyday tasks. What began as a small community initiative quickly grew into a vision for a platform that could help seniors everywhere stay connected, safe, and independent.

Today, Companio brings together hundreds of seniors and local businesses — creating a network of care and companionship that spans generations.
